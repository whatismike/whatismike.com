DEADHORSE
oil on canvas
2014-15
price upon request


Meet DEADHORSE, a series of 16 original oil paintings by Brooklyn-based visual artist Mike Rose. Completed after nearly a year of work, deadhorse was exhibited at Greenpoint Gallery in New York City in March, 2015.


The artist shot photographs of everyday objects in industrial New York through antique glass bottles salvaged from Dead Horse Bay, then translated the results to oil. The process incorporates history, abstraction, and the urban environment. Each of the 16 canvases represents a uniquely ephemeral view of the City’s present as viewed through a remnant of its past.


The canvases are arranged in a grid pattern that resembles a windowpane. The grid has been installed in 5’x 5’ and 6’ x 6’ arrangements, dependent on spacing. The title of each piece is a numerical code that reflects the day and time on which the base photograph was taken.
